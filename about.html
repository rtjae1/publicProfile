ABOUT

Hello, and welcome to the world of Ryan. I am very new to web development, so be kind! My name is Ryan and I've been around since 1984. I was born in Richmond, Virgina but moved quickly to Tampa, Florida. I attended Jesuit High School while in Tampa. After high school I moved to the suburbs of Philadelphia to attend Villanova University. I've bounced around the mainline ever since.

At Villanova I majored in Psychology and Mathematics. With capstone projects on Timothy Leary and the Collatz Conjecture. Since university I have spent time on very different career paths. Right away I began in insurance, working on Long Term Disability products. After 6 years of that, I moved on and began working for a mobile canning company.

At the top of this page, you'll find a link to my portfolio, as well as an option to contact me with any questions, comments, and feedback! Clicking on my name, or the About link, will always bring you back to this page right here.
